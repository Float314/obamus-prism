## Barack Obama

Barack Obama is an American politician who served as the 44th President of the United States from 2009 to 2017. He was the first African American president in U.S. history. 

Before becoming president, he worked as a lawyer and served as a U.S. Senator from Illinois. During his presidency, he focused on issues such as healthcare reform, economic recovery after the 2008 financial crisis, and international diplomacy.

He is widely known for his speeches and efforts to promote unity and change.
